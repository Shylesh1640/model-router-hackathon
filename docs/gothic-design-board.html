Design Principles
Weight
Everything is heavy or it's nothing. Unbounded 800 for headings, never below 400 weight. Monolithic blocks, not delicate layers.
No Curves
Zero rounded corners. Not one. Brutalism doesn't curve. The only radius in the system is 0px.
Depth Through Value
No shadows, no gradients. Depth comes from contrast: #000 → #050505 → #0C0C0C → #1A1A1A.
One Accent
If everything is accent, nothing is. Tier colors are desaturated — just enough to distinguish at a glance.
